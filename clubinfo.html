Club Compass

Animal Rights & Climate Justice

Location: The Pa
Meetings: Wednesdays at 5:30pm

We take action in a variety of ways on campus to promote veganism and a plant-based food system for the sake of animals and the climate. This includes hosting educational events, workshops, and campaigns to raise awareness about animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and the benefits of plant-based living. We collaborate with students, local businesses, and organizations to create a supportive community, provide resources for transitioning to veganism, and advocate for systemic change. If you’re passionate about animal rights, climate justice, or simply want to learn more about sustainable food choices, don’t hesitate to reach out and get involved with our initiatives.
